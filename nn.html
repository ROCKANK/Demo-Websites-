About Us

Building since 1999 — one honest deal at a time.

New Navkar Properties & Builders has spent over 25 years turning plots into homes and listings into closed deals. What started as a small self-construction practice has grown into a trusted name for both property dealing and full-scale self-construction.

Led by Devender Jain and Samkit Jain, our approach stays the same as it did on day one: no middlemen games, no hidden costs, and construction quality we'd put our own name on — because we do, on every project we hand over.
