About Shreem Art Studio

A small studio, poured by hand.

Shreem Art Studio began with a simple pour and a love for colour — now it's a one-artist studio in Hyderabad turning resin into keychains, magnets, frames, and keepsakes that people actually want to hold on to.

Every piece is mixed and set by hand, which means no two pours are identical — and your piece, whatever the occasion, is made for you specifically, not pulled off a shelf.
