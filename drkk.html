About Dr. Kartikay

Making everyone see better.

Trained at AIIMS Delhi, Dr. Kartikay has spent his career on two things at once: performing precise, modern eye surgery, and teaching people how their eyes actually work — through public explainers, resident pearls, and everyday consultations.

Whether it's a routine number check, a second opinion on a diagnosis, or planning a cataract or LASIK procedure, every patient gets the same AIIMS-trained attention to detail — in person or over video.
