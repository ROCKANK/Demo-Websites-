Our approach

Three disciplines. One membership.

Most studios make you choose between lifting heavy and slowing down. We built Forma so you don't have to — every membership moves across all three rooms.

01 — Move

Functional movement

Mobility-first sessions that restore range before we load it. The room you start in, not the one you outgrow.

02 — Lift

Strength training

Small-group barbell and kettlebell programming, coached in groups of eight or fewer so form is never guessed at.

03 — Rest

Breath & stillness

Closing the week with breath-led recovery sessions — because adaptation happens in rest, not just in reps.
