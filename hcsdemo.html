Our techniques

DHI or FUE — we'll help you choose.

Both are proven hair transplant methods. The right one depends on your hair loss pattern, donor area, and goals — something we walk through together at your free consultation.

FUE
Follicular Unit Extraction

Individual follicles are extracted and implanted one by one. A well-established, widely trusted technique with a strong track record.

No linear scarring
Suitable for larger sessions
Shorter recovery than strip methods
Often Preferred
DHI
Direct Hair Implantation

Follicles are implanted directly using a specialized pen, without pre-making incisions — allowing tighter control over angle, depth, and density.

Higher precision placement
Minimal handling of grafts
Natural-looking density and direction
Real results
